Work with us

Our team is our strength and pride. At Soul Savera, we’re committed to creating a workspace that our team looks forward to each morning—where bright, respectful, supportive, creative, and fun-loving colleagues come together to make a difference.

We are a team of compassionate individuals who share common values, ethics, and a strong commitment to our cause. Our bonds extend far beyond the walls of our workspace, and we believe in continual growth through shared learning and training opportunities.

We’re always on the lookout for passionate psychologists, occupational therapists, pediatric physiotherapists, special educators, teachers, and other professionals dedicated to working with children. If you’re driven to make an impact in mental health and developmental support, we’d love to hear from you!

How to Apply:
Just send us an email at connect@soulsavera.com with a cover letter and CV, and we will contact you as soon as we have an opportunity for you!

Be a Part of Soul Savera

At Soul Savera, we value the incredible contributions of volunteers who help us make a difference in mental health and developmental support. By volunteering with us, you’ll join a compassionate team dedicated to creating a positive impact for children, adolescents, and families.

Why Volunteer With Us?

As a volunteer, you’ll assist with therapy sessions, facilitate workshops, and support our outreach programs. You’ll gain hands-on experience, learn new skills, and be part of a team that believes in inclusion, empowerment, and resilience.

Why Volunteer With Us?

As a volunteer, you’ll assist with therapy sessions, facilitate workshops, and support our outreach programs. You’ll gain hands-on experience, learn new skills, and be part of a team that believes in inclusion, empowerment, and resilience.

Who are we looking for?

Passionate individuals interested in mental health, education, and developmental care who are eager to learn and grow alongside our team.

How to Apply:
Send your CV and a brief cover letter to connect@soulsavera.com. Join us in building a future where sensitivity is strength and every individual finds their true potential!

Requirements: Currently pursuing a minimum of a Bachelor’s degree in Psychology, Occupational Therapy, Physiotherapy, Special Education, or related fields in child development.

As an intern at Soul Savera, you will have the opportunity to:

  • Observe therapy sessions and gain exposure to various therapeutic approaches throughout the day.
  • Assist in our in-house programs and gain hands-on experience with children in real-world settings.
  • Facilitate activities within sessions and contribute to events held at Soul Savera.
  • Collaborate on creating content for Soul Savera’s social media, sharing insights and resources.
  • Gain practical experience in interventions for neurodevelopmental differences, under the supervision of experienced in-house therapists.
  • If you are passionate about mental health, child development, and making a meaningful impact, we invite you to apply and join our team!

How to Apply:
Just send us an email at connect@soulsavera.com about yourself and your area of interest and we will contact you as soon as we have an opportunity for you!

Collaborate with Soul Savera: Making a Bigger Impact Together

At Soul Savera, we believe in the power of collaboration to drive transformative change. We welcome professionals and organizations passionate about mental health and inclusivity to join hands with us.

Why Collaborate With Us?

Whether you’re a psychologist, occupational therapist, speech therapist, special educator, or pediatric physiotherapist, your expertise can enhance our initiatives. Collaborators co-develop workshops, therapy programs, and awareness campaigns to expand our reach and impact.

How to Collaborate:
Send us an email at connect@soulsavera.com with your proposal or area of interest. Together, we can redefine mental health and developmental support, creating a world where everyone thrives
