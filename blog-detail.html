Dr. Sujatha Sharma
M. Phil. (Gold medalist), & Ph.D. (Clinical Psychology from NIMHANS, Bangalore)
Experience: 32+ Years in Healthcare
About

Sujatha D. Sharma, M.Phil., Ph.D., is a practicing Consultant Clinical Psychologist in New Delhi and has been working with all age groups, with psychological distress and emotional well-being issues for the last three decades. She has a multifaceted background as clinician, psychotherapist, researcher, teacher and trainer.

Her consulting and training specialties include training workshops for promoting wellness behavior, stress management, life skills for children and parenting; handling relationships, public education, awareness, trauma counseling and spiritual counseling.

Dr. Tamanna Saxena
Associate Professor with Amity Institute of Psychology and Allied Sciences,
Amity University, Uttar Pradesh, Noida, India
Experience: 17+ Years of Teaching and Research
About

Dr. Tamanna Saxena has an experience of more than 20 years. She has been earlier associated with University of Delhi, NIPCCD, Ministry of Women and Child Development, India. Along with that she has worked on Research Projects of World Health Organization, University of Minnesota, U.S.A.

Being a Child Development expert, her areas of Interest are Moral Development, Cognition, Spirituality, Play and Play Therapy in Children. She has her Doctorate in Psychology on the topic “Spirituality and Wisdom as predictors for Moral Development in Adolescents”. She is UGC-NET qualified. She teaches courses pertaining to Therapies such Art and Play Therapy and Expressive Art Therapies, Forensic Mental Health, LifeSpan Development, Special Education and College and Career Counselling to Graduate, Post Graduate Students and PhD scholars in India.

Dr. Sandeep Katevarapu
MSC (Dual), MA (Dual), PsyD, PhD
Experience: 12+ Years
Founder and Chief Managing Director at We Avec U®
Founder Director at We Avec U Organization LLC (USA)
About

Dr. Sandeep Katevarapu is a distinguished psychologist, educator, and visionary leader with over 12 years of expertise in clinical, neuro, and forensic psychology. Through his unwavering dedication to mental health, he has delivered more than 700 motivational lectures, provided psychological care to over 4,000 patients, and inspired countless individuals to prioritize mental well-being and resilience.

Dr. Katevarapu’s impact extends beyond individual care. Over the past decade, through We Avec U Foundation, he has reached more than 25,000 people with psychoeducation initiatives, workshops, and advocacy programs aimed at fostering mental health awareness and education. His efforts have profoundly influenced communities by equipping them with the tools and knowledge to address psychological challenges.

With 8 years of teaching experience, Dr. Katevarapu has shaped the academic and professional growth of students in psychology. As a visiting professor at numerous prestigious universities, he continues to contribute to academic excellence and innovation. His work is globally recognized, with numerous awards and research publications that highlight his expertise and commitment to advancing psychological science.

Dr. Katevarapu is also a prolific contributor to the global academic community, having presented research at over 125 international conferences and organized 14 major conferences that foster collaboration and knowledge exchange in the mental health field.

As the Founder and President of We Avec U Trust, We Avec U Pvt. Ltd., and We Avec U Organization LLC (USA), Dr. Katevarapu has established these entities as leading advocates for mental health awareness and education. His leadership and vision have transformed the landscape of mental health services, making them more accessible and impactful.

Dr. Katevarapu’s work embodies a mission to empower individuals and communities through education, research, and innovative solutions. His tireless efforts continue to redefine the boundaries of mental health care and leave a lasting legacy of transformation and hope.
